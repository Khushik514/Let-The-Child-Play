The Corona Monster

The corona monster has taken over the planet. There is a state of panic every where.
This monster is hurting every innocent human being around you and is trying to cast its rule over planet Earth.
Our parents, teachers, friends and guardians, all are in danger and you need to save them from this dangerous virus aka monster.
So Get Ready To Fight!!

How to fight the monster?

This monster is very dangerous and harms anyone who comes in close contact with it. We need to stop the contact between the monster and the people in order to save them from getting harmed.
There are various ways to stay away from the virus.
Wanna learn more? Read ahead!

Stay at Home

This is the first way to fight the virus. Everyone should stay at home and be safe.
The safest place right now is home and it is the best way to fight the virus as well.

Social Distancing

The coronavirus can spread from one person to another, like when we share toys or hold a friend’s hand. Right now, it’s safer not to have play dates with friends. You may be home from school for a while, too.
Although you can’t visit your friends, you can still see each other in other ways: with your parents’ permission, you can talk on the phone or over video chat. You can also stay in touch as pen pals by writing letters or postcards.

Wash your hands

Keeping your hands clean will help you and your family avoid getting sick and spreading germs to other people.
Wash your hands for 20seconds atleast, with soap and water.

Wear a mask

If you’re going out in public, like to the grocery store, you may see lots of people wearing masks over their faces. Don’t be scared: People are doing this as another way to help keep each other safe.
The masks help protect the person wearing them from breathing in germs or, if they are sick, from spreading the germs to others.
If you see someone not wearing a mask, ask them to wear it.

Cover while coughing and sneezing

Coronavirus germs can travel through coughs and sneezes. That’s why it’s important to cover your nose and mouth with your elbow or a tissue when you cough or sneeze.
Wash your hands afterwards, and, if you use a tissue, make sure to throw it away.

Help your parents

Because your parents may be working from home now, they may be busy, tired or stressed-out.
Give them a hand by helping with chores, like setting the table or cleaning up your toys when you are done playing with them.

Don't be scared

Like all other monsters, this virus will come to an end too.
So don't worry too much and give the courage you have to people around you so that they can also fight with the virus nicely.
